=== R2 Gaming Brainstorming ===

--- Feature Ideas ---
- In-app tournament brackets that update live
- "Squad" system for rural teams to build a local identity
- Achievement badges for "First Win", "Top 10", "Regional Finalist"
- Profile "Signature" for gaming IDs across multiple titles (Free Fire, BGMI, etc.)
- SMS notifications for match times (useful for low data users)

--- AI / Future Tech ---
- Basic performance analysis: "Your early game is strong, but you struggle in late zones"
- Automated matchmaking that prioritizes local latency over MMR
- Coaching suggestions based on common mouse/touch movement patterns
- Identifying hidden talent by cross-referencing stats across multiple local tournaments

--- Monetization ---
- Entry fees for premium tournaments with larger prize pools
- Local brand sponsorships (FMCG, mobile shops, local tech brands)
- Tiered membership: Pro vs. Amateur paths
- Selling data insights on grassroots trends to larger esports orgs?

--- Expansion ---
- R2 Academy: A scholarship program for the top 1% of rural players
- Local streaming hub: Highlighting rural tournaments with local commentary
- Offline tournament "Kiosks" in semi-urban gaming cafes

--- Risks & Uncertainties ---
- Will rural players trust an online-only payment/registration system?
- Mobile gaming dominates; a web-first platform might be ignored (need a mobile app ASAP?)
- Can we actually prevent cheating without expensive anti-cheat software?
- Electricity and internet outages during high-stakes matches could kill regular participation
- Difficulty in verifying user age and identity in remote locations

--- Possible Failure Points ---
- Burn rate exceeds sponsorship intake too early
- Platform scalability fails when 500+ users join a bracket at once
- Lack of local "Hero" players makes the platform feel stagnant
- Oversaturation from big metropolitan tournaments stealing interest
- High friction during registration (too many fields, complex UI)
